# 🚀 Smart Supply Chain: Delivery Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
In today's competitive e-commerce landscape, delivery delays can severely damage customer satisfaction and tie up capital. This project analyzes the **DataCo Supply Chain dataset** to identify bottlenecks, uncover the root causes of delayed deliveries, and provide actionable recommendations to optimize operations.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **SQL:** Data extraction, cleaning, and aggregation (Calculating delay durations and revenue at risk).
* **Tableau:** Designing an interactive, dynamic dashboard for visual root-cause analysis.
* **Canva / PowerPoint:** Crafting a professional business presentation to communicate insights.

## 📊 Key Business Insights
After analyzing the data, the following critical bottlenecks were identified:
1. **The Shipping Paradox:** "Standard Class" shipping accounts for the highest volume of delays (~15,000 orders), but "Second Class" shipping is the most inefficient, causing the longest average delay of **2.5 days** per order.
2. **Product-Level Vulnerability:** The **Cleats** and **Men's Footwear** categories suffer the most, with over 11,200 delayed shipments combined.
3. **Geographic Risk:** Western Europe and Central America are the primary operational pain points for late deliveries.
4. **Financial Impact:** Over **36,048 orders** were late, putting approximately **$20.1 Million** of revenue at risk.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
* **Renegotiate Carrier SLAs:** Immediately review contracts with carriers handling "Second Class" shipping to enforce stricter penalties for delays.
* **Supply Chain Audit:** Conduct a fulfillment audit specifically for the "Cleats" department to eliminate internal warehouse bottlenecks.
